Swiggy Partner Online Chennai

step 1: Contact Swiggy To partner with Swiggy, the restaurant has to leave an enquiry on the organization’s Partner Registration Webpage.
Step 2: Connecting with a Sales Representative A sales representative from Swiggy will connect with the applicants.
Step 3: Meeting with Sales Representative A Representative of Swiggy will meet the applicant in person to determine their demands and other requirements.
Step 4: Agreeing with Terms and Conditions The applicant and the sales representatives must enter into an agreement.
Step 5: Gathering of Records After agreeing to the terms, a few fundamental records are gathered and dissected within a couple of days.
Step 6: Becoming a partner After the completion of these formalities, the restaurants will be conferred as partners of Swiggy.

Documents Required
The following are the documents required to become a Swiggy partner.
Document portraying FSSAI Registration
Partnership PAN Card.

A copy of GST Registration.and the tax details.
Details of the proprietors.
Commission Charged by Swiggy
Swiggy charges a commission of 15%-25% in developing markets such as Bengaluru and in places where an eatery is new to the local community. A commission of 16% must be remitted to Swiggy by the restaurant. Meanwhile, a commission of 4%-7% must be incurred to promote a specific restaurant.
