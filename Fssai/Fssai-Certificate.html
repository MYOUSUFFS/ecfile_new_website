REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE


Fssai Registration Certificate gives big relief to small food operator where they can register their business in Fssai for Rs.100 per year and Maximum Rs.500 for 5Years, Fssai manages and checks for secure manufacturing, storage and handling practices of FBOs (Food Business Operators). Simple Registration is called Form A .

FSSAI registration are mandatory before starting any food business. It is a 14-digit registration number to the food operators . Every manufacturer and trader or even a small food stall operator , who indulge in any sort of food business should get Fssai Regsitration Certificate.

The FSSAI Registration Certificate is the Basic for small businesses or startups which usually have turnover less than Rs.12 Lakhs.

This registration gives the customers a secure feeling the food that they consumed is of good quality.
FSSAI Registration helps in decreasing the chances of adulteration in food products and substandard products.

FSSAI Registration Certificate is valid from one year to maximum 5years.
To get FSSAI registration certificate, Fssai has formulates certain conditions and procedures to get it registered in their portal. you need to compile with the Fssai procedures and need to do to certain arrangements in your premises as required. This depends what type of food business you do.

Documents for Registration Certificate
1. Food operators Passport size photo self Attested
2. Identity proof( pancard / Aadhaar card/Voter's ID) 3. Address proof (Electricity Bill or Rental Agreement)

FSO Inspection
Food Safety Offivce is the competent authority to inspect your premises and instruct the necessary arrangement to comply with FSSAI norms.
FSO has to power to approve your registration or decline your Registration

Registration Certificate validity
1.Regsitration issued by Fssai is Valid for aperiod time but it should be renewed before 30days from the date of Expire.
2.Any Regsitration is not Renewed in stipulated period og time then Fssai will a penalty of Rs.100 per Day.

Modification of Registration Certificate
1.Registration Certificate issued by Fssai can be modified, If the jurisdition and scope is same
2.Any change in Jurisdition or scope of business, Fssai will charge you modification fee of Rs.100 per Year.
